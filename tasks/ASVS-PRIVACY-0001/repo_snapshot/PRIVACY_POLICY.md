# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## User Data Rights

Users of our platform have the right to request deletion of their personal data at any time. This policy governs how we handle such requests.

### Right to be Forgotten

Upon receiving a deletion request from a user, we commit to removing all personal data associated with that user within 30 days. This includes:

- User account information (name, email, contact details)
- User-uploaded files and associated metadata
- Any data that directly identifies the user

### Data Minimization and Anonymization

While we delete personal identifiers and files, certain operational logs must be preserved for legitimate business purposes such as security analysis, fraud detection, and service analytics. However, these logs must be anonymized to protect user privacy.

**Activity logs containing personally identifiable information (PII)** must be processed as follows:

- The `userId` field must be replaced with a non-reversible hash
- All other PII fields (usernames, email addresses) must be removed
- Non-PII fields such as action type, timestamp, and numeric amounts must be preserved for analytics

The log entry itself is retained in our systems, but the user is no longer identifiable from it.

### Completion Timeline

Deletion requests must be completed within 30 days of receipt. We acknowledge all deletion requests and confirm completion when all personal data has been removed.

### Exceptions to Deletion

Certain categories of records may have legal or regulatory requirements that prevent complete deletion. See our Data Retention Policy for details on records that must be preserved.
